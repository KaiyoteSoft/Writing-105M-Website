The Decline of Boxing

A colorful collage

In this colorful inDesign newspaper page I worked with two other people to comeplete the project. Jared Stanley supplied the text, shortening and lengthening the story in order to fit the page. Catherine Yu was responsible for drawing much of the clipart you see. I was responsible for the general layout and placement of the various graphics and text. Additionally, I selected and edited the items such as the boxing ring and various objects in Photoshop before including them in the page. All decisions about the nuances of the page such as text wrapping, page color, pull quote graphics, font type, and style were made by me.
I chose to display this page because it showcases many of my inDesign abilities. However, I also chose to it because it proves that I can manage a team well and come up with a finished product. We completed this page in under eighteen hours, well before the deadline.

The "One and Done" Rule

Overcoming Obstacles

This page utilizes an elegant, simple graphics that supplement the actual story. While most people can create meaningless, but impressive looking images in Photoshop, few can choose appropiate graphics to supplement the story. And even fewer can choose those graphics if there is no story to reference.
When I began designing this page, the story was unwritten and I had only the title. Based off the words "One and Done" and the cryptic descriptions of what the story would be, I was required to choose a layout and finish the page in three days. Just before the end of the third day the text would be added and I would make the final tweaks.
To add onto the mounting problems, my page partner was obsessesed with perfection. I would stand up to get food only to come back and see that the changes I had been working on for three hours were gone and my partner was redoing the layout. Nevertheless I stayed calm and worked with this person to finish the page on time.
We ended up finishing on time. Furthermore, the editors decided that this design was one of the best layouts of the year. However, this page is an example of my ability to work through trying situations and towards the goal.

The New Face of Paly Athletics

The Complete Package

This was my first story and page design as a Sports editor. To asses my ability the editors gave me the opportunity to write, photograph, and design all the necessary aspects of my page. The most distiguishing part of this piece is actually the interaction I had with the subject of the article. Over the course of three weeks I interviewed this teacher and attempted to get pictures. I had to be polite and often patient with her schedule. Ultimately, this story speaks to my ability not just as a designer, but also as a journalist.
